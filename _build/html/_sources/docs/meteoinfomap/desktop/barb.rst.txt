************************
Barb
************************

The wind field will be plotted as barbs according U and V weights.

.. image:: ../../../_static/meteoinfo/grid_barb.png

After select ‘ColorVar’ option, the wind barbs could be colored by third various selected in 
‘Var’ item.

.. image:: ../../../_static/meteoinfo/grid_colorbarb.png
